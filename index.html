Legacy of Giving

The TUEAA Endowed Scholarship Fund

Since 2012, TUEAA members have come together to build a permanent endowment that funds scholarships for Tuskegee engineering students. Corporate matching gifts, annual giving, and planned gifts all count toward our $1 million goal. Every dollar you invest is an investment in the next generation of Tuskegee engineers.
